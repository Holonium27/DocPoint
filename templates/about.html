about us

we take care of your healthy life

Doc Point is an application made to streamline the process of finding doctors online. We have taken inspiration from many of the successful doctor-search products out there, and have isolated the core features we think are the most important to people in need of services in remote areas, and with need for particular timings. We also target specialties of our affiliated doctor so that you may rest assured that we have found the right doctor for your issues.
